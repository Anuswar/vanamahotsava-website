about event

Vana Mahotsavam, also known as the Forest Festival, is an annual celebration dedicated to the spirit of environmental conservation and sustainable living.

This tradition, deeply rooted in our values at Shri Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management, marks a significant effort to contribute to the well-being of planet.

Vana Mahotsavam holds a special place in our hearts as it goes beyond a mere event. It's a collective endeavor to raise awareness about the vital role of trees and forests in maintaining ecological balance.

The event is a testament to our commitment to environmental stewardship and a greener future.

event schedule

Time Genre Description
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM Registration and Welcome Refreshments Gather at the registration desk, grab your event kit, and enjoy some refreshing snacks as we prepare for a day of green festivities.
9:00 AM - 9:30 AM Inaugural Ceremony Join us for the official opening of Vana Mahotsavam 2023. Experience inspiring speeches, a traditional lamp-lighting ceremony, and the unveiling of this year's environmental theme.
9:30 AM - 10:30 AM Tree Plantation Drive - Session 1 Get your hands dirty and contribute to our tree plantation drive. Plant saplings, learn about the significance of each tree, and bond with fellow participants as we sow the seeds of a greener future.
10:30 AM - 11:00 AM Refreshment Break Recharge with some light refreshments. Network with like-minded individuals, visit the information booths, and explore the beauty of the event venue.
11:00 AM - 12:00 PM Cultural Performances Immerse yourself in the beauty of cultural expressions celebrating nature. Enjoy dance, music, and art performances that resonate with the theme of environmental conservation.
12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Lunch Break Indulge in a delicious lunch spread with eco-friendly practices. Connect with fellow participants and share experiences while enjoying a meal amidst nature.
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Guest Speaker Session Engage with experts in environmental conservation. Learn from insightful discussions, presentations, and Q&A sessions with our esteemed guest speakers.
2:00 PM - 3:00 PM Tree Plantation Drive - Session 2 Continue our tree planting efforts with another interactive session. Contribute to increasing the green cover and leave a lasting impact on the environment.
3:00 PM - 3:30 PM Closing Ceremony and Thank You Wrap up the day with a closing ceremony, expressing gratitude to all participants, sponsors, and contributors. Share highlights from the event and reinforce our collective commitment to a sustainable future.
3:30 PM - 4:00 PM Networking and Socializing Wind down the day by connecting with fellow participants, sharing experiences, and building lasting connections. Enjoy light snacks as we conclude Vana Mahotsavam 2023 on a positive note.
